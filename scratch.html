Fly Fishing

A passion on the water

Why I Love Fly Fishing

There is something about standing in a cold mountain river, reading the water, and presenting a hand-tied fly to a wild trout that no other sport can match. It combines patience, skill, and an intimate connection with nature.

Gear Basics

Getting started doesn't have to be expensive. Here's what you need:

  1. Rod & Reel
    • 5-weight rod is the most versatile
    • Pair with a matching reel and floating line
  2. Flies
    • Dry flies (float on surface)
    • Nymphs (fish below surface)
    • Streamers (mimic baitfish)
  3. Waders & Boots
    • Felt-sole boots for rocky riverbeds
    • Breathable waders for warm weather
  4. Other Essentials
    • Polarized sunglasses (see into the water)
    • Forceps to remove hooks safely
    • Fishing license — always!
